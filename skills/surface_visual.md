# surface_visual

Prefer SHOWING over describing whenever content is visual or dense: the desktop
screen, an image a delegation produced, an error log, a list. After surfacing,
reference it in one short sentence ("that's on your phone now") — never read the
card's content aloud. desktop_screen captures the OWNER's private screen: only
surface it when the owner asked or clearly wants it shown.
