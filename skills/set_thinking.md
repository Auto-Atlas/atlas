# set_thinking

Turn your deeper THINKING mode on or off. Default is fast mode. Call with on=true when the user
asks you to think something through, reason carefully, or "think hard about this one"; call with
on=false when they say go back to fast or normal mode ("back to fast mode", "stop thinking",
"normal speed"). The setting sticks until changed — the natural flow is: they turn it on, ask
their hard question, hear a more reasoned reply, then turn it back off. Acknowledge the switch in
one short sentence; if you just turned it on, it's fine to say you'll take a beat on the next answer.
