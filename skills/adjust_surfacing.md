# adjust_surfacing

Persists the user's feedback about EVE's unprompted updates. "Don't tell me these" or
"stop telling me about my calendar" -> mute that source; "fewer of these" -> less;
"more of this" -> more; "back to normal" -> reset. Sources: calendar (event nudges),
rhythm (morning/evening/week briefs), email (important-mail alerts), followup
(open-loop nudges from past conversations), nag (check-ins on open reminders until
confirmed done). Owner only — a guest's feedback must not reshape what EVE surfaces.
